Support for Parents

We know how overwhelming it can be to seek help. That’s why we’ve designed our intake process to be simple, warm, and judgment-free.

What to Expect

  • Initial consultation with a family therapist
  • Personalized care plan based on your child’s needs
  • Ongoing support through workshops and check-ins

FAQs

Do I need a referral? No, you can contact us directly.

What ages do you support? Typically ages 2–14, but we assess each case individually.
